Our Work

An assistive digital tool using text-to-speech technology to read aloud prescription and medical record information. Using MediReader, take or upload a picture of your medical record or prescription, and the text will be coverted to an audio recording that will be played back to the user.

Our Work

It is built to enhance patient care by helping patients, the elderly population and those with vision impairments and/or reading difficulties understand or remember their medical prescription information.
