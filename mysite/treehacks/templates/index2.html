Our Work

An assistive digital tool using text-to-speech technology to read aloud prescription and medical record information. Using MedReader, take or upload a picture of your medical record or prescription, and the text will be converted to an audio recording that will be played back to the patient.

Why

It is built to enhance patient care by helping patients, the elderly population and those with vision impairments and/or reading difficulties understand or remember their medical prescription information.

How

Using Optical Character Recognition and text-to-speech technology, medical prescription information can be extracted and converted to audio messages for users.
